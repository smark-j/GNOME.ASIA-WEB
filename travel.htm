Getting to Chongqing

Chongqing is well-connected for both national and international travellers.

You can check this link to learn more about Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chongqing_Jiangbei_International_Airport

And you can visit this site to check flights to Chongqing http://english.ctrip.com

We try to provide one-time airport pick-up service for speakers at Chongqing Airport at 2PM, Oct 13, 2017, if you need us to pick up you from airport, please feel free to contact gnome@chongqinglug.orgwith mail title “CQ Airport pick-up on GNOME.Asia”

Book Hotel & Getting to Hotel
