ABOUT

GNOME.ASIA SUMMIT

GNOME.Asia Summit is the featured annual GNOME Conference in Asia. The event brings together the GNOME community in Asia to provide a forum for users, developers, foundation leaders, governments and businesses to discuss the present technology and future developments.

https://wiki.gnome.org/GnomeAsia/

CHONG QING LUG

CHONG QING LUG is one of the local organizers of GNOME.Asia summit 2017. Chongqing LUG has nearly 500 local members and over 10 years of significant experience in organizing 20 successful events.

https://chongqinglug.org/

LINUX STORY

LINUX STORY is one of the local organizers of GNOME.Asia summit 2017. With the fully support of 6000 contributors, Linux Story has been becoming one of the most popular news media of open source in mainland China.

https://linuxstory.org/
